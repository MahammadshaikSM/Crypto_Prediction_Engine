About CryptoTracker Pro

A real-time crypto performance tracker built for long-term insight accumulation.

Data Source

CoinGecko free public API — tracks top 200 coins by market cap with 24h and 7-day performance data. A free demo key from coingecko.com/en/api gives the best experience.

Auto-Refresh

Data refreshes automatically every 12 hours. Hit Refresh any time for an immediate update.

History & Calendar

Each daily snapshot is stored locally in your browser's localStorage — up to 90 days. The Calendar tab lets you browse any day the app was open. Data persists across browser sessions on the same machine.

Dashboard Sections

  • 24h Performance — ranked by % change in the last 24 hours
  • 7-Day Performance — ranked by weekly % (CoinGecko only)
  • Dollar Amount Movers — ranked by absolute USD price move
  • Volume Leaders — ranked by 24h trading volume

Quantitative Analysis

Next Week Prediction

Top 10 coins most likely to outperform next week, based on momentum signals.

∑
Scoring Formula
5-factor quantitative model (v2)
Score = 0.30 × VolAdjMomentum + 0.20 × VolumeSurge + 0.18 × MomentumAccel + 0.17 × BTCRelative + 0.15 × RSI Score
VolAdjMomentum 30%
7-day momentum divided by the coin's own volatility. A coin up 20% with low volatility is a stronger signal than one up 20% with wild swings — this factor rewards steady trending moves.
7d% ÷ (std-dev(daily returns) × 100 + 1)
VolumeSurge 20%
Volume-to-MarketCap ratio, normalized (0→100). High relative volume signals institutional or retail conviction — price moves on high volume are more likely to sustain.
normalize(volume / marketCap) × 100
MomentumAccel 18%
Is today's pace faster than the weekly average? If the coin gained 14% over 7 days (~2%/day avg) but is up 5% today, momentum is accelerating — a bullish signal.
clamp(24h% ÷ (7d% ÷ 7), 0, 3) ÷ 3 × 100
BTCRelative 17%
How much the coin outperforms Bitcoin over 7 days. In a bull market everything rises — the real alpha is coins beating BTC. Positive = outperforming Bitcoin; negative = underperforming.
normalize(7d% − BTC 7d%)
RSI Score 15%
RSI(14) calculated from 168 hourly sparkline prices. Rewards under-extended coins (RSI 30–60) and heavily penalises overbought coins (RSI ≥ 70). Avoids buying tops.
RSI≥80→0 · RSI≥70→20 · RSI≥60→50 · RSI≥40→70 · RSI≥30→85 · RSI<30→100
⚠️ Confidence cap at 72%. This model uses momentum, volume, and RSI signals derived from CoinGecko sparkline data. It does not account for macroeconomic events, regulatory news, project fundamentals, or broader market sentiment. Treat predictions as one data point among many — never as financial advice.
